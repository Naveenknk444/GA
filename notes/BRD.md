# Recovery Community App — Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Vision

Create a free, anonymous recovery platform that helps compulsive gamblers
connect with recovery, meetings, literature, and other recovering gamblers.

The goal is not to maximize users, engagement, or revenue. The goal is:

> To help people avoid the suffering caused by gambling addiction and make
> recovery support available whenever gambling is available.

## Problem Statement

Modern gambling is available 24/7 through smartphones — sports betting apps,
casinos, advertisements, promotions. Many people struggle during late nights,
periods between meetings, moments of isolation, and times when they hesitate to
call someone. Recovery support should be equally accessible.

## Guiding Principles

- **Recovery First** — every feature must support recovery.
- **Anonymity** — no real names, no profile pictures, no phone numbers.
- **Service** — members help one another.
- **No Gambling Promotion** — no strategies, picks, wins/losses, or dollar amounts.
- **Community Over Technology** — technology connects people; people help people.

---

## Phase 1 (MVP)

**Goal:** Validate that recovering compulsive gamblers will use a mobile
platform to connect and support each other.

1. **Home Screen** — tiles: Talk, Meetings, Recovery, Share.
2. **Community (Talk)** — create posts (Discussion / Support / Milestone),
   comment, view a chronological feed.
3. **Share** — share experience, ask questions, request support, celebrate milestones.
4. **Meetings** — search by state / city / zip; view name, address, time, directions.
5. **Recovery Resources** — GA literature, newcomer resources, Gamban install,
   self-exclusion resources.
6. **Notifications** — optional; support requests, replies, meeting updates.
7. **Community Rules** — not allowed: gambling amounts, wins, losses, betting
   strategies, casino promotion.

**Success criteria:** users create posts, respond to support requests, find
meetings, and access recovery resources.

---

## Phase 2

**Goal:** Improve connections and recovery support through intelligent assistance.

1. **AI Categorization** — classify posts (Urge / Relapse / Support / Question / Milestone).
2. **AI Content Review** — detect gambling amounts, promotion, rule violations; suggest better wording.
3. **Literature Matching** — suggest relevant literature and recovery stories (references, not advice).
4. **Smart Support Matching** — connect users with similar experiences and relevant resources.
5. **Meeting Communities** — private meeting groups (e.g. Harrisburg GA), membership approval, announcements, local discussions.
6. **Enhanced Notifications** — member needs support, new discussion in group, meeting updates.

> Note: AI features are planned to run on **Azure AI**, with app data synced there.

---

## Explicitly Out of Scope

The app will NOT: act as a sponsor, act as a therapist, provide recovery advice,
replace meetings, replace the GA program, sell gambling-related products, or use
popularity systems.

No followers, likes, influencers, or user rankings.

## Mission Statement

> A free, anonymous recovery community that helps compulsive gamblers connect
> with recovery, meetings, literature, and one another while respecting the
> principles of anonymity, unity, and service.
