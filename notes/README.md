## Guiding principles (from the BRD — these constrain every feature)

- **Anonymity** — no real names, no profile pictures, no phone numbers
- **Recovery first** — every feature must support recovery
- **No gambling promotion** — no amounts, wins, losses, or betting strategies
- **No popularity systems** — no followers, likes, influencers, or rankings
- The app is **not** a sponsor, therapist, or a replacement for the GA program
